My Hobbies

Here are some of my favorite hobbies and activities.

  • Playing video games - I enjoy diving into a variety of genres to suit my mood.

    • I particularly love first-person shooters like CS2 and Valorant for their adrenaline rush and stragetic gameplay, or Insurgency(2014) and Battlefield for more slow-paced experience.

    Sometimes, I also plays some zombie games such as Left 4 Dead and No More Room in Hell for their survival elements, and sandbox games like Minecraft that allow creative freedom and exploration.

    When I'm looking for something more relaxed, I turn to casual games such as Stardew Valley or Power Wash Simulator that offer a pleasant distraction without requiring intense focus or time commitment.

  • Programming - A passion of mine, primarily driven by the joy of creating and exploring my own capabilities through personal projects.

    • I love diving into coding challenges just for fun, experimenting with different languages and frameworks to see what I can build. Whether it's a quirky utility script in Python, a small web application using JavaScript, or a game project using C# in Unity, my motivation comes from the excitement of turning an idea into a working solution.

    I enjoy pushing my boundaries, learning new technologies, and discovering the extent of my programming skills through personal side projects that challenge and inspire me.
